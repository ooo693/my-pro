      <h3 class="lunch-title">Lunch</h3>
      <h3>Healthy Pizza with Vegetable Soup</h3>

      <div class="ingredients">
        <h4>Ingredients:</h4>
        <p><strong>For the Pizza:</strong></p>
        <ul>
          <li>1 whole wheat tortilla or thin flatbread</li>
          <li>2 tablespoons tomato sauce</li>
          <li>1/4 cup shredded low-fat mozzarella</li>
          <li>Vegetable toppings: bell peppers, mushrooms, olives, onions</li>
          <li>A pinch of dried oregano or basil</li>
        </ul>
        <p><strong>For the Vegetable Soup:</strong></p>
        <ul>
          <li>1 chopped carrot</li>
          <li>1/2 cup chopped zucchini</li>
          <li>1/2 cup spinach or kale</li>
          <li>1/4 cup chopped onion</li>
          <li>1 garlic clove, minced</li>
          <li>2 cups vegetable broth</li>
          <li>Salt, pepper, and herbs to taste</li>
        </ul>
      </div>

      <div class="instructions">
        <h4>Instructions:</h4>
        <ol>
          <li>Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Spread tomato sauce on the flatbread, add toppings and cheese, then bake for 10 minutes.</li>
          <li>In a pot, sauté onion and garlic until fragrant. Add vegetables and broth.</li>
          <li>Let it simmer for 15–20 minutes until vegetables are tender. Season to taste.</li>
          <li>Serve the pizza hot with a warm bowl of vegetable soup.</li>
        </ol>
      </div>

      <div class="instructions">
        <h4>Serving Suggestion:</h4>
        <p>Enjoy this light and balanced meal with a glass of sparkling water with lemon slices.</p>
