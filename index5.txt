      <h3 class="breakfast-title">Breakfast</h3>
      <h3>Healthy Egg Sandwich with Arugula</h3>

      <div class="ingredients">
        <h4>Ingredients:</h4>
        <ul>
          <li>2 slices of whole wheat or oat bread</li>
          <li>1 egg (boiled or fried without oil)</li>
          <li>A handful of fresh arugula (or substitute with lettuce)</li>
          <li>Cucumber slices</li>
          <li>Tomato slices</li>
          <li>A pinch of black pepper or sumac (optional)</li>
          <li>A drizzle of olive oil (optional)</li>
        </ul>
      </div>

      <div class="instructions">
        <h4>Instructions:</h4>
        <ol>
          <li>Toast the bread lightly in a pan or toaster until slightly crispy.</li>
          <li>Place the arugula on the first slice of bread.</li>
          <li>Add the egg on top of the arugula (slice it if boiled).</li>
          <li>Add the cucumber and tomato slices on top of the egg.</li>
          <li>Sprinkle black pepper or sumac for extra flavor. You can also add a small drizzle of olive oil if you like.</li>
          <li>Top with the second slice of bread, press gently, and serve immediately.</li>
        </ol>
      </div>

      <div class="instructions">
        <h4>Serving suggestion:</h4>
        <p>Serve the sandwich with a cup of yogurt or a glass of fresh orange juice for a healthy, balanced breakfast.</p>
