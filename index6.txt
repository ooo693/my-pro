      <h3>White Cheese Toast with Veggies</h3>

      <div class="ingredients">
        <h4>Ingredients:</h4>
        <ul>
          <li>2 slices of whole wheat toast</li>
          <li>2 tablespoons low-fat white cheese (like feta or soft halloumi)</li>
          <li>1/2 tomato, sliced</li>
          <li>Sliced cucumber</li>
          <li>Lettuce or arugula leaves</li>
          <li>A sprinkle of thyme or oregano (optional)</li>
          <li>Light drizzle of olive oil (optional)</li>
        </ul>
      </div>

      <div class="instructions">
        <h4>Instructions:</h4>
        <ol>
          <li>Lightly toast the bread until warm and crisp.</li>
          <li>Spread the cheese on one slice of toast.</li>
          <li>Add tomato slices, cucumber, and lettuce leaves.</li>
          <li>Sprinkle thyme or oregano and drizzle olive oil if desired.</li>
          <li>Top with the second slice of toast, press gently, and serve immediately.</li>
        </ol>
      </div>

      <div class="instructions">
        <h4>Serving suggestion:</h4>
        <p>Serve with a cup of yogurt or warm mint tea for a satisfying and balanced breakfast.</p>
