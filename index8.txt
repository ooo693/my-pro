    <div class="meal-content">
      <h3 class="meal-title">Bone Broth with Rice and Vegetables</h3>

      <div class="ingredients">
        <h4>Ingredients:</h4>
        <ul>
          <li>1 kg meat bones (preferably with marrow, like thigh bones or fatty bones)</li>
          <li>2 large onions, chopped</li>
          <li>2 carrots, chopped</li>
          <li>2 celery stalks (optional), chopped</li>
          <li>2 cloves garlic, minced</li>
          <li>2 bay leaves</li>
          <li>1 tsp black pepper</li>
          <li>1 tsp salt (or to taste)</li>
          <li>1.5 liters water (or as needed)</li>
          <li>1 cup Basmati rice or long-grain rice</li>
          <li>1 cup mixed vegetables (like green beans, peas, potatoes, or zucchini)</li>
          <li>1 tbsp olive oil (optional)</li>
        </ul>
      </div>

      <div class="instructions">
        <h4>Instructions:</h4>
        <ol>
          <li>In a large pot, add the bones and cover with cold water.</li>
          <li>Bring to a boil, then remove the scum that forms on the surface.</li>
          <li>Add the onions, carrots, celery, garlic, bay leaves, black pepper, and salt.</li>
          <li>Lower the heat and let it simmer for 4-6 hours, until the broth is rich and flavorful.</li>
          <li>In a separate pot, wash the rice under cold water and cook it with 2 cups of the bone broth (after straining the broth).</li>
          <li>In a pan, heat the olive oil (optional) and saut√© the vegetables until tender (about 7-10 minutes).</li>
          <li>Once the broth is ready, strain it and pour it over the rice and vegetables. Serve warm.</li>
        </ol>
      </div>

      <div class="instructions">
        <h4>Serving Suggestion:</h4>
        <p>Serve this dish with fresh herbs like parsley or dill, or add a squeeze of lemon for an extra burst of flavor.</p>
