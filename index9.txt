      <h3>Grilled Chicken Chickpea Salad</h3>

      <div class="ingredients">
        <h4>Ingredients:</h4>
        <ul>
          <li>Grilled chicken breast, sliced</li>
          <li>1 cup cooked chickpeas</li>
          <li>Chopped cucumber</li>
          <li>Cherry tomatoes or chopped regular tomatoes</li>
          <li>Fresh lettuce or spinach leaves</li>
          <li>Juice of half a lemon</li>
          <li>1 tablespoon olive oil</li>
          <li>Salt and black pepper to taste</li>
          <li>A pinch of cumin (optional)</li>
        </ul>
      </div>

      <div class="instructions">
        <h4>Instructions:</h4>
        <ol>
          <li>In a large bowl, combine the chickpeas, cucumber, tomatoes, and greens.</li>
          <li>Add the grilled chicken slices on top.</li>
          <li>In a small bowl, mix the lemon juice, olive oil, salt, pepper, and cumin (if using).</li>
          <li>Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently.</li>
          <li>Serve immediately as a healthy and satisfying lunch.</li>
        </ol>
      </div>

      <div class="instructions">
        <h4>Serving suggestion:</h4>
        <p>Pair with a glass of cold water infused with lemon and mint for a refreshing touch.</p>
